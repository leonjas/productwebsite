Eco-waterball is designed to replace the millions of plastic bottles thrown away every year. The water ball is a biodegradable and natural membrane which can be fully swallowed and digested, as well as hydrating people in the same way as drinking water. Water drinks on the go is the major advantage of these edible balls.

The product is made from algae (sea weed) and is edible materials and tasteless, although flavours can be added to it. Drinking water from inside a soft edible membrane made from natural seaweed extract is considered as a sustainable product in the long run.
